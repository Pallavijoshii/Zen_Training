The Byke Heritage,

The Byke Heritage Pure vegetarian resort in Matheran – is Matheran’s most luxurious resort offering you enchanting cottages nestled amidst the green hills of this quaint hill station. Spread across an expansive 7 acres, The Byke Heritage, Matheran Bungalow is the first property to be built by the discoverer of Matheran Mr. Huge Malet, the then collector of Thane in 1854. This Resort is a heritage property which stands majestically in all its pristine glory. The Byke Heritage, Pure vegetarian resort in Matheran is equipped with modern day amenities to ensure comfort, ease and convenience, each of our cottages are designed to charm and pamper you with luxury. Enjoy fine dining at our multi-cuisine vegetarian restaurant, as our chefs tickle your taste buds with delicious delicacies. Sip your favourite liquid, as we treat you to a fine selection of alcoholic and nonalcoholic beverages.Take a break from the hustle and bustle of city life, as you jump into our Olympic size swimming pool, or revitalize yourself at our spa and massage parlour. Enjoy an evening with family and friends as you grove to the music at our in-house discotheque.
