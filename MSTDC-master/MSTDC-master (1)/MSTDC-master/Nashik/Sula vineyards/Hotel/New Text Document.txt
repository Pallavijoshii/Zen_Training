Hotel Panchvati yatra
Set 1 km from 1700s Naroshankar Temple, this unpretentious hotel is also 1 km from 19th-century Kalaram Temple and 10 km from Nasik Road train station. 

Modest rooms offer free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and minifridges. Upgraded rooms add air-conditioning. Suites feature separate living areas. Room service is available 24/7.

Breakfast is complimentary. There's also a vegetarian restaurant and a casual eatery with a terrace. Other amenities include a bar, an exercise room and meeting space. Parking is available.
