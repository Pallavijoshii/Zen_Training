Hotel details
Off the Mumbai Nashik Expressway, this unpretentious budget hotel is 7 km from the ancient Nasik Caves and 13 km from Sula Vineyards. 

No-nonsense rooms offer complimentary Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs. Upgraded rooms add air-conditioning, and some have minibars. Children age 4 and under stay at no extra cost with an adult.

Parking is free, and breakfast is available for a fee. There's also a low-key vegetarian restaurant.
