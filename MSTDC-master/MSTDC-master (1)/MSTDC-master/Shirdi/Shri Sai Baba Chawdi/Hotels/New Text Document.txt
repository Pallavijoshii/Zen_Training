Hotel Sai Suraj Park offers 3-star accommodation in Shirdi. It provides free Wi-Fi, a car rental desk and laundry facilities. 

The hotel has 26 rooms and has been recently refurbished. Staff are available 24 hours a day and can assist with booking tours and tickets. 

Hotel Sai Suraj Park is close to Sri Shirdi Sai Baba Temple. Guests can also visit Wet n Joy Water Park.
