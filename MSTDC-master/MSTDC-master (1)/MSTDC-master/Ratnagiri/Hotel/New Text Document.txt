Hotel Navalai Pride is a top-rated and trendy hotel located close to the Ratnagiri Railway Station and the famous Light House on the edge of the city overlooking the endless waters of the Arabian Sea. The massive rooms have huge windows which offer a great view of the surrounding forests. The ample space in the room has been wisely used to accommodate a standard seating area and a massive wardrobe. The management offers a bunch of amenities to all the guests which include an air-conditioner, TV, and Wi-Fi services in every room. The campus is equipped with CCTV and 24/7 power backup that ensures a safe and comfortable stay for the guests. The hotel is within the walking distance of some popular restaurants such as Sanskruti Restaurant, Rajasthani Restaurant and Masala Kitchen.
