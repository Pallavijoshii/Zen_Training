A 12-minute walk from Pune Junction train station, this unassuming hotel is 1.8 km from the 18th-century Shaniwar Wada fort, and 11 km from Pune Airport. 

Featuring free Wi-Fi, the subdued rooms also have flat-screen TVs, desks and ceiling fans. Upgraded rooms add air-conditioning. Room service is available 24/7.

Parking is complimentary, and there’s a generator on-site. An airport shuttle is offered, and pets are welcome, both for a fee.
