Situated in Lonavala, 6 km from Bhushi Dam, Hotel Mangal Residency features a garden and views of the garden. Each accommodation at the 3-star hotel has city views, and guests can enjoy access to a children's playground. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk, airport transfers, room service and free WiFi.

All rooms at the hotel are fitted with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a shower.

Guests at Hotel Mangal Residency can enjoy a Full English/Irish or a vegetarian breakfast.

Lion's Point is 12 km from the accommodation, while Tiger Point is 12 km from the property. The nearest airport is Pune International Airport, 69 km from Hotel Mangal Residency.
