Upvan Lake is located in Thane in the Indian state of Maharashtra. It is known for hosting the Sanskruti Arts Festival. It was founded and reconstructed by J.K. Singhania for water supply. Singhania also constructed the lord Ganesha's temple at Upvan lake.
