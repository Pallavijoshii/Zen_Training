Tikuji-Ni-Wadi is an amusement park, water park, and a resort near Mumbai and at Thane. It is located at about 15 kilometres from Mumbai. The Water Park has pools and water slides. The amusement park includes go-karts, roller coasters, giant wheels and water park.
