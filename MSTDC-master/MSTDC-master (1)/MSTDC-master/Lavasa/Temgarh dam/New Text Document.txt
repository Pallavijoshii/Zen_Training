About Temghar Dam
Situated on the Mutha River flowing through Pune city is the beautiful Temghar Dam. It is located at a distance of 40 km from Pune in Lavasa. One can take a drive from Pune to Lavasa to treat their eyes with the enticing panorama around the Dam. 

One can relish a bhutta along with tea on his/her visit to Dam. The feeling of staying proximal to the Dam, coupled with the blend of natural splendor is a beautiful feeling. For those visiting Lavasa, Temghar Dam must be on the wish list.
