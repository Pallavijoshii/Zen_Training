Celebration Lavasa is a 4-star property situated in Lavasa. Featuring a bar, the 4-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with a private bathroom. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk and room service for guests.

At the hotel, rooms have a desk and a flat-screen TV. The rooms will provide guests with a wardrobe and a kettle.

A continental breakfast is available each morning at Celebration Lavasa.

The accommodation offers a children's playground.

The nearest airport is Pune International Airport, 85 km from Celebration Lavasa.
